- Pronunciation: "Sam-Alt-Mem" (three syllables)

## Tagline

"**Some Alt Translation Memory**" - Build TMs from fuzzy matches and MT suggestions

## What It Does

SamAltmem extracts alternative translations (alt-trans) from XLIFF files and converts them to TMX format, helping translators build comprehensive translation memories from fuzzy matches, machine translation suggestions, and other alternative translations.
